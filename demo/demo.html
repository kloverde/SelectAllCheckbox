Group 4 (disabled checkboxes)

The state of disabled checkboxes are unaffected by the select-all checkbox. Their default states, whether checked or unchecked, remain - and they contribute to the status of the overall checkbox group. Note that the select-all checkbox never displays as unchecked - it's either checked or indeterminate. This is because there is a disabled, checked checkbox.
